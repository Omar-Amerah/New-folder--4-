    Wiring help

    Draw: drag through occupied ship cells. Drawing Power over existing cable changes traversed sections to the selected tier; same-tier cable is reused.

    Erase: removes the selected physical section. Inspect: hover for live Power flow and watch energy pulse outward from its source; click only to open the detailed analysis.

    Capacity: sustained is safe continuous load; peak is the delivery ceiling. Power cable above sustained generates extra Heat and overload stress.

    Display: Light is pale yellow, Standard vivid orange, and Heavy deep copper-red; they also use increasing thickness. Green is working, yellow loaded, orange above sustained, red at peak, red dashed disconnected, and blue selected.

    Power vs Data: Power carries energy and uses tiers. Data is a separate single-tier network with no capacity or overload mechanics; overlap never silently deletes the other network.
